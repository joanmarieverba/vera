Supernatural Jane Austen

Vera Nazarian's hilarious and surprisingly romantic Supernatural Jane Austen Series is a supernatural "mash-up" series of parody novels of the entire oeuvre of Jane Austen.

Visit the official Supernatural Jane Austen Series website for more information on these delightful and comical Regency fantasies, available now from Curiosities, an imprint of Norilana Books.

Current books in the series:

  • Mansfield Park and Mummies: Monster Mayhem, Matrimony, Ancient Curses, True Love, and Other Dire Delights (2009) by Jane Austen and Vera Nazarian
  • Northanger Abbey and Angels and Dragons (2010)
  • Pride and Platypus: Mr. Darcy's Dreadful Secret (2012)

Forthcoming books:

  • Pagan Persuasion: All Olympus Descends on Regency
  • Emma Enchanted
  • Sense and Sanguine Sensibility
  • Lady Susan, Succubus
