Essays and Nonfiction

Essays, Articles, and Non-Fiction:

  • "Publicity and Self-Promotion Nouveau: Doing It With Class," article on publicity and self-promotion for writers, appearing in Speculations Issue Forty-Six, edited by Susan Fry, April 2002
  • "Home Improvement in Magic Land," pop culture essay appearing in the BenBella Books SmartPop Series anthology Totally Charmed: Demons, Whitelighters and the Power of Three based on the TV show Charmed, edited by Jennifer Crusie, November 2005.
  • "Death's Got Your Back -- When Edward and the Big Bad Heavies Are on Your Team," Ardeur: 14 Writers on the Anita Blake, Vampire Hunter Series (Smart Pop series) edited by Laurell K. Hamilton, March 2, 2010.
  • "A Kinder, Gentler Vampire," A Taste of True Blood: The Fangbanger's Guide (Smart Pop) edited by Leah Wilson, June 8, 2010.
  • "You're My Obsession," A Visitor's Guide to Mystic Falls: Your Favorite Authors on The Vampire Diaries edited by Leah Wilson, Red And Vee of Vampire-Diaries.net, October 5, 2010.
