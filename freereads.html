Free Reads

Good news! Many of Vera Nazarian's stories and other works are available to read for free online.  You can find some of them listed below:

 

Novels

  • Cobweb Bride (Cobweb Bride Trilogy, Book One), Leda, an imprint of Norilana Books, July 15, 2013. Complete novel is free on Amazon, Barnes & Noble NOOK Book, Smashwords, Apple iBooks, and Kobo.
  • Qualify (The Atlantis Grail, Book One), Norilana Books, December 20, 2014. Complete novel is free on Amazon, Barnes & Noble NOOK Book, Smashwords, Apple iBooks, Kobo, All Romance Ebooks, and Noise Trade.

  • Port Custodial Blues," Helix #2, October 2006 (WSFA Small Press Award 2007 Finalist)
  • "Niola's Last Stand," Black Gate Magazine, edited by John O'Neill, May 12, 2013.
